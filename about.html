About Sunrise Medical Center

Caring for The Gambia's health since 2010

Our Story

Sunrise Medical Center was established with a vision to provide accessible, quality healthcare to the people of The Gambia Founded by Dr. Ousman Jatta in 2010, our clinic has grown from a small practice to one of Banjul's most trusted medical facilities.

We believe that everyone deserves access to quality healthcare. Our mission is to provide compassionate, professional medical services to all patients, regarddless of their background.

Our Facilities

Our modern clinic includes:

  • Confortable consultation rooms
  • Well-equipped treatment area
  • On-site laboratory for basic tests
  • Pharmacy stocked with essential medicines
  • Clean and welcoming waiting area

Meet Our Doctors

Our experienced medical team is dedicated to your health and well-being

Dr. Ousman Jatta

Specialty:General Medicine

Dr. Jatta is the founder and lead physician at Sunrise Medical Center. With over 20 years of experience, he has dedicated his career to improving healthcare access in The Gambia. He completed his medical training in Dakar and London.

Dr. Fatou Njie

Specialty:Pediatrics & Women's Health

Dr. Njie joined our team in 2015 and specializes in children's health and women's wellness. She is known for her gentle approach and dedication to preventive care. She trained at the University of The Gambia Medical School
